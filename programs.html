Welfare Initiatives

Focus Areas & Service

Detailed look at how we run our continuous programs to deliver service (सेवा) on the ground.

Focus Area 01

Primary Education Support

We believe that primary education is the key to creating positive परिवर्तन. Many children drop out of rural schools because they cannot afford basic supplies. Our program distributes high-quality notebooks, durable school bags, stationery, and drawing kits before school opens every year.

Success Highlight: Supplied comprehensive school kits to over 75 school-going children in rural blocks of Nagpur and surrounding zones for the current academic session.

Focus Area 02

Free Rural Medical Camps

Many villagers have to travel hours to consult a doctor, leading to undiagnosed health concerns. We organize free medical checkup camps in coordination with professional volunteer doctors. We provide blood pressure, diabetes, eye checkups, and distribute basic prescribed medicines and pediatric supplements.

Success Highlight: Screened over 120 patients during our last medical checkup drive, providing prescriptions and free essential medicine kits to daily wagers and seniors.

Focus Area 03

Food Security & Ration Distribution

Hunger is a major obstacle for daily wage workers. Under our community assistance drives, we run food distributions providing nutritious freshly cooked meals. We also distribute dry monthly grocery kits containing flour, cooking oil, rice, lentils, and basic spices to low-income families.

Success Highlight: Distributed over 45 dry ration kits supporting daily wager households for a full month and served hot meals to 200+ road-side dwellers.
300+
Persons Served
45+
Families Assisted
Focus Area 04

Clothing & Blanket Distribution

Severe weather is a serious risk for children and seniors sleeping in uninsulated shelter facilities or urban slums. Zanzavat coordinates winter and monsoon clothing drives. We distribute warm blankets, sweaters, school uniforms, and raincoats to children to ensure their safety and comfort.

Success Highlight: Gathered and distributed 100+ warm blankets and winter wear to roadside families during cold waves in late December.

Blankets Given
50+

Focus Area 05

Student Welfare & Child Development

Empowering kids requires more than just academic materials. We coordinate student welfare programs focused on primary health, hygiene education, creative art classes, and basic values workshops. We aim to inspire a strong संकल्प in each child to learn and succeed.

Success Highlight: Conducted primary hygiene workshops in 2 village communities, distributing soap kits, toothbrushes, and running wellness quizzes.
