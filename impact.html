Overview

Tracking Three Decades of Grassroots Change

Since 1995, Zanzavat Bahuudeshiya Shaikshanik Sanstha has steadily expanded its footprint. What started with basic local educational classes has grown into systematic regional community support. We work closely with school administrators, doctors, and village heads to identify children and families facing critical resource deficits and deliver high-impact programs directly to them.

By measuring our reach, we hold ourselves accountable to our donors, volunteers, and the community. Behind every number is a story of positive परिवर्तन.

Achievements

Our Footprint of Service

A summary of the programs and reach we've conducted under our focus areas.

Education Sponsorship

Every year before school opens, we supply complete kits containing durable school bags, primary notebooks, compass boxes, and drawing kits to over 75 children, reducing dropout rates.

75+
Active Pupils

Primary Healthcare

Partnering with volunteer general practitioners and pediatricians, we hold free camps focusing on children’s wellness, basic eye testing, and distribute vitamins and primary medicines.

120+
Screened in Last Camp

Food & Grocery Support

During festivals and seasonal shifts, we run food distribution programs serving hot meals and distribute dry monthly ration boxes containing rice, lentils, flour, and oil to daily wagers.
