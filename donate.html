How Your Support Helps

Your Compassion Empowers Families

Every contribution to Zanzavat Bahuudeshiya Shaikshanik Sanstha has a direct effect. Because we are a registered society operating with volunteers, we keep our operational expenses low, ensuring that your funds are directed directly to purchasing program resources.

Sponsorship Levels

₹1,000 • Educate a Student: Funds a complete school kit containing a sturdy bag, notebooks, compass box, drawing supplies, and stationery.
₹2,500 • Medicate a Community: Sponsors primary medicines, vitamin drops, eye drop kits, and doctor fees for free medical checkup camps.
₹5,000 • Feed 10 Families: Funds monthly ration grocery bags (flour, oil, rice, lentils) for daily wagers or homeless roadside dwellers.

🛡️ Safe & Transparent Donations

Zanzavat Bahuudeshiya Shaikshanik Sanstha is a registered NGO under the Societies Registration Act. We provide official receipts for all monetary contributions. For certificate inquiries, contact us at zanzavatsanstha@gmail.com.
